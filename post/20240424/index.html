Invited Session Proposal Accepted for the ITSC 2024

Apr 24, 2024 1 min read
Image credit: ITSC
The proposed invited session "AI-Enhanced Safety-Certifiable Autonomous Vehicles" has been accepted for the Our work on robustly complete stochastic abstractions was presented at the 27th IEEE International Conference on Intelligent Transportation Systems (ITSC), which will be held from September 24 to 27, 2024, in Edmonton, Canada.
