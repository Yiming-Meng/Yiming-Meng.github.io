Opportunities

Openings: Students with strong self-motivation are welcome to apply to the Ph.D. or MPhil programs (See application instructions for the PhD and MPhil programs). Applicants may have strength in, or a strong interest in exploring, either theoretical aspects (e.g., control theory, functional analysis, partial differential equations and their numerical methods, stochastic processes, or scientific machine learning) or application-driven work in systems control, autonomy, learning, and algorithm development. Opportunities are also available for RA, undergraduate students, postdoctoral researchers, and visiting researchers.

Prospective students and researchers are welcome to email me with their CV, transcripts, and a brief statement of research interests. Applicants are encouraged to familiarize themselves with my research themes (see details) and clearly articulate how their interests align.
